What is Fine Tuner?

Fine Tuner is a chromatic tuner for iOS with a minimalistic user interface designed specifically for string instruments. It utilizes your device's built-in microphone.

Multiple Available Tunings

The app includes over 30 popular guitar tunings as well as tunings for the following instruments: bass (4,5 & 6 string), ukulele, banjo, violin, viola and cello.

Fine Tuner also has the ability to tune to a custom note and thus can be used to tune almost any instrument.

Universal App

Fine Tuner is a universal app. Once you get it, you can use it on any iOS device: iPhone, iPad or iPod.
