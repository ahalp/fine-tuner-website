Privacy Policy

Mobile App

Fine Tuner uses Google Analytics to collect traffic data. This includes data such as user interactions within the app, devices used and location (granularity is limited to city). The data is not personally identifiable. Fine Tuner uses Google Analytics purely to determine the usage of the different areas of the app. This will allow me to determine which areas of the app I should target for future updates. Crashlytics is also used to to send back logs in the event of a crash. This includes information about the device the crash occured on (ie. iOS version, orientation, system usage). This information is used to allow me to respond to crashes as they happen.

This Website

This website uses Google Analytics to collect traffic data. This includes data such as pages visited, time spent in the different areas of the website and visitor location (granularity is limited to city). The data is not personally identifiable. This information is collected purely out of personal interest regarding the website's usage.
